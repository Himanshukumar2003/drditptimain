Our Services

Smile Makeover Veneers

Dental veneers are thin, custom-made shells of tooth-colored material placed over the front surface of teeth to improve their appearance, correcting issues like chipped, stained, or crooked teeth Veneers are used in cosmetic dentistry to correct a variety of dental issues, including: Discolored Teeth: Fixing severe discoloration or uneven coloring that can't be addressed with whitening. Chipped or Broken Teeth: Restoring the appearance of teeth that have been damaged. Misaligned or Worn Teeth: Improving the shape and appearance of teeth that are worn down or oddly shaped. Gaps Between Teeth: Closing gaps to create a more uniform smile.

Aligners

The Invisalign® System is the most advanced clear aligner system in the world, trusted by over 20.1 million people worldwide to improve their smiles. SmartTrack™ material. Align Technology’s own patented aligner material that provides gentle, more constant forces to improve control of tooth movements. After testing 260 different materials, our material on average enables faster treatment. SmartStage™ technology. Programming each tooth movement in a certain sequence, at the right time to achieve optimal outcomes and greater predictability. SmartForce™ attachments. Attachments and features designed into the aligners deliver the precise forces when needed to achieve more predictable tooth movements. The Invisalign® System is the only system in the world with features such as Bite Ramps, Precision Cuts, Power Ridge to help address a wide range of clinical situations.
